Why Keeping a Permanent Marker in Your Bathroom is the Ultimate Travel Hack

www.apartmenttherapy.com · Sat, 18 Jul 2026 15:30:00 -0400

Keeping a permanent marker in the bathroom is an easy, cost-effective hack that simplifies travel organization by allowing immediate labeling of toiletries and tracking of expiration dates.

Keeping a simple permanent marker, such as a Sharpie, in your bathroom cabinet might seem unusual at first, but it is quickly becoming recognized as a game-changing travel and organizational hack. The primary purpose of this simple trick is to label toiletries and personal care items immediately upon opening them. By writing the opening date directly on the bottle, travelers and homeowners alike can easily track the expiration dates of their cosmetics, sunscreens, and skincare products, ensuring they never accidentally pack expired or ineffective items for their upcoming trips.

Beyond tracking expiration dates, keeping a permanent marker within arm's reach in the bathroom simplifies the packing process for frequent travelers. It allows individuals to clearly label travel-sized silicone bottles with their specific contents, easily distinguishing between shampoo, conditioner, body wash, or specialized facial cleansers. This prevents any frustrating mix-ups in dimly lit hotel bathrooms. Additionally, marking the fill line or writing brief usage instructions on prescription bottles or decanted liquids helps maintain a seamless, stress-free daily routine while away from home.

This low-cost, high-efficiency organization system drastically reduces clutter and eliminates the guesswork often associated with packing and personal hygiene. Instead of searching through desk drawers for a pen when frantically preparing for a journey, having a dedicated marker in the bathroom establishes a consistent habit of immediate labeling. Ultimately, this minor and effortless adjustment to your daily household routine ensures that your travel kits remain perfectly organized, safe, and fully prepared for any adventure at a moment's notice.
