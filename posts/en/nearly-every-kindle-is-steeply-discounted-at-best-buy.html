Best Buy Drops Prices Across Nearly All Amazon Kindle Models in Summer Sale

www.theverge.com · 2026-07-21T13:13:23-04:00

Best Buy has discounted nearly all Amazon Kindle models as part of its Black Friday in July sale, bringing the Kindle Paperwhite back to its record-low price of $124.99.

Best Buy has launched a major promotional campaign as part of its promotional "Black Friday in July" sales event, offering significant discounts across almost the entire lineup of Amazon Kindle e-readers. Consumers looking to purchase a reliable digital reading device ahead of the upcoming school year or for late-summer travel can take advantage of some of the lowest prices seen this year. Among the most notable featured deals is the highly popular Kindle Paperwhite, which has officially returned to its all-time low price of $124.99, representing a substantial $35 discount from its regular retail price.

The promotional event covers a wide range of Kindle models designed to suit different reading preferences and budget levels. The standard Kindle Paperwhite remains a standout option for readers due to its crisp display, adjustable warm light features, water resistance, and exceptionally long battery life, making it a widely recommended choice for everyday use. In addition to the mid-tier Paperwhite, shoppers can also find appealing price cuts on entry-level Kindles as well as higher-end premium devices, providing an opportune moment for both first-time buyers and long-time users seeking a modern upgrade.

Major retail events like this often trigger temporary price drops across popular tech products, offering consumers a rare mid-year opportunity to secure holiday-level savings well ahead of the traditional winter shopping season. Interested shoppers are encouraged to review the available discounts promptly, as promotional electronics sales typically feature limited stock and strict time limits. Whether preparing for academic studies, personal reading, or upcoming travel plans, these significant current markdowns make acquiring a feature-packed Amazon e-reader far more accessible to budget-conscious buyers.
