Critics Choice Association Launches Inaugural Creator Awards for Digital Storytellers and Podcasters

www.forbes.com · Fri, 24 Jul 2026 08:32:30 -0400

The Critics Choice Association has launched the inaugural Critics Choice Creator Awards to honor digital creators across podcasts, web series, and diverse online content platforms.

The Critics Choice Association (CCA) has officially announced the launch of its inaugural Critics Choice Creator Awards, a major new initiative aimed at recognizing and celebrating outstanding talent within the digital space. Designed to honor digital creators across podcasts, online series, and diverse forms of internet content, this new honors system reflects the changing landscape of modern entertainment. As audience consumption shifts increasingly toward digital platforms, the association aims to validate and elevate the creative efforts of independent and commercial online storytellers.

The new awards program will evaluate a wide array of digital programming, paying special attention to originality, production quality, and cultural impact. By creating categories dedicated specifically to podcasters, vloggers, and digital narrative producers, the CCA is building a dedicated bridge between traditional critical acclaim and the fast-paced realm of online media. Industry experts and critics within the association will serve as evaluators, ensuring that exceptional digital projects receive the professional recognition they deserve alongside traditional film and television releases.

This initiative represents a significant milestone for digital creators who have long operated outside the boundaries of conventional Hollywood award circuits. The Critics Choice Association plans to share additional details regarding submission guidelines, specific award categories, voting procedures, and the official date for the inaugural ceremony in subsequent announcements. Ultimately, the Critics Choice Creator Awards aim to establish a respected standard for digital media excellence while inspiring the next generation of online creators worldwide.
